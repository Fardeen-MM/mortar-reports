The Playbook

How to Close Each Gap

Here's exactly how to fix each gap yourself—no gatekeeping. Fair warning: marketing isn't a one-time project. It's ongoing. These steps don't stop.

01

Get Found — Paid Advertising

The Problem: You're invisible when founders search at 11 PM.

🛠️ DIY Fix
Setup (One-Time)
1 Create Google Ads account, set up conversion tracking
2 Research keywords: "startup lawyer SF," "fractional GC," "VC financing attorney"
3 Build 3-5 ad groups by client type (founders, creatives, investors)
4 Create dedicated landing pages for each practice area
Weekly (Forever)
5 Review search terms, add negatives for irrelevant queries
6 Create fresh ad creative—platforms reward new content weekly
7 Monitor competitor ads, test new angles
⏱️ Setup: 15-20 hrs
🔄 Ongoing: 8-12 hrs/week FOREVER
✨ We Handle All of This
  • Keyword research (500+ terms analyzed)
  • Campaign architecture & ad group structure
  • Ad copywriting—3-5 variations per group
  • Landing page design & optimization
  • Conversion tracking setup & monitoring
  • Negative keyword management (weekly)
  • Bid adjustments & budget optimization
  • Fresh ad creative (weekly)
  • A/B testing headlines, CTAs, images
  • Competitor ad monitoring
  • Monthly performance reporting
  • Quarterly strategy reviews

You approve the budget and take the consultations. That's it.

⚠️ Reality check: Ad creative dies fast. What worked last week won't work next month. You need fresh creative EVERY WEEK to stay competitive.
02

Get Answered — 24/7 Intake

The Problem: 35%+ of inquiries come after hours. Voicemail loses 85% of them.

🛠️ DIY Fix
Setup (One-Time)
1 Research answering services (Ruby, Smith.ai, LEX Reception)
2 Write intake scripts for founders, creatives, investors
3 Configure call forwarding and lead delivery
Monthly (Forever)
4 Review call recordings, update scripts
5 Retrain staff when turnover happens (expect every 3-6 months)
⏱️ Setup: 8-12 hrs
🔄 Ongoing: 2-4 hrs/week + retraining cycles
✨ We Handle All of This
  • AI receptionist setup & configuration
  • Custom intake scripts for your practice areas
  • Qualification logic (urgency, matter type, budget)
  • Calendar integration for direct booking
  • CRM integration for lead delivery
  • 30+ language support
  • <5 minute response time, 24/7/365
  • Call recording & transcription
  • Lead scoring & prioritization
  • Ongoing optimization & improvements
  • No retraining when "staff" turns over (AI doesn't quit)

You take qualified appointments. That's it.

⚠️ Reality check: Human services have turnover. Every time someone leaves, you're back to square one on training. AI never quits.

04

Get Closed — CRM & Follow-Up

The Problem: Leads go cold because follow-up is manual and inconsistent.

🛠️ DIY Fix
Setup (One-Time)
1 Choose a CRM (Clio Grow, Lawmatics, HubSpot)
2 Map your intake workflow: lead → qualification → consult → client
3 Build automated email sequences (immediate, 24hr, 72hr, 7-day)
Daily (Forever)
4 Check new leads—respond within 5 minutes during business hours
5 Complete follow-up tasks, update lead statuses
⏱️ Setup: 15-25 hrs
🔄 Ongoing: 5-8 hrs/week FOREVER
✨ We Handle All of This
  • CRM setup & configuration
  • Intake workflow mapping
  • Automated email sequences
  • SMS follow-up automation
  • Task & reminder system setup
  • Lead source tracking
  • Pipeline reporting & analytics
  • Dead lead reactivation campaigns
  • Template creation & optimization
  • Ongoing sequence refinement
  • Monthly conversion analysis

Leads get nurtured automatically. You just show up to consultations.

⚠️ Reality check: CRM only works if you work it. Every day. Skip a week and leads go cold.

You can absolutely do this yourself—many successful firms do. But be honest about the time commitment: this isn't a weekend project. It's 20-35 hours/week of ongoing work, forever. That's a part-time job on top of teaching at Stanford and running your practice.
