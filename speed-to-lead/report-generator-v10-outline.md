# Report Generator V10 - Structure

## 17 Sections (NO urgency banner):

1. Header (Logo, Prepared For, Date)
2. Hero (Big opportunity number, Week 1 results)
3. Scorecard (Visual X/10 checklist)
4. Competitor Comparison Table (5 firms, all data)
5. Keyword Opportunities Table (10 keywords, volume, CPC, rank #1)
6. Website Audit Findings (PageSpeed, issues, specific checks)
7. Google Business Profile Audit (reviews, photos, categories)
8. Ad Analysis (running ads?, competitor examples, spend estimates)
9. Local SEO Status (Map 3-pack, citations, directories)
10. "What's Happening Right Now" (4 pain scenarios)
11. Revenue Gaps with Detailed Math (each gap broken down)
12. Mid-page CTA
13. Solutions with Deliverables Timeline
14. 90-Day Timeline with Milestones
15. Case Study (practice-area matched)
16. FAQ (5 questions, objection handling)
17. Final CTA with "What You Get on the Call"

All with deep explanations and massive value.
