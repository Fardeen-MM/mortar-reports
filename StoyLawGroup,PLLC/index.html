1Create Google Ads account, set up billing
2Research keywords: "truck accident lawyer Fort Worth" + 500 variations
3Build campaign structure (3-5 ad groups by practice area)
4Write 3+ ad variations per group

6Set up conversion tracking (calls, forms, consults)
7Configure negative keywords to block junk traffic
8Review search terms weekly, pause losers, scale winners
9Write fresh ad copy weekly (ad fatigue is real)
10Adjust bids by location, time, device—weekly
