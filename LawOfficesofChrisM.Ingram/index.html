01
Get Found — Fix Ads + Launch Meta
3 of 4 Google campaigns underperforming. Zero Meta presence reaching STEM and entertainment professionals.
🛠️ DIY Fix
1
Audit all 4 Google Ads campaigns — pause the 3 underperforming ones
2
Research keywords: "EB-1 lawyer Los Angeles," "O-1 visa attorney California," "E-2 business visa" + variations
3
Rebuild campaign structure: separate ad groups for EB-1, O-1, E-2, H-1B, and family-based
4
Write 3 ad variations per group with specific visa language
5
Create dedicated landing pages per visa type (not the homepage)
6
Set up Meta Business Manager, install Pixel (it's already on the site), build audiences
7
Create Meta campaigns targeting STEM professionals, entertainment industry workers, entrepreneurs
⏱️ Setup: 15-25 hours 🔄 Ongoing: 8-12 hours/week FOREVER ⚠️ Ad creative dies fast. New ads needed EVERY WEEK.
✨ We Handle All of This:
  • Full audit of existing 4 Google Ads campaigns
  • Keyword research (500+ immigration-specific terms)
  • Campaign restructure by visa category
  • Ad copywriting — 3-5 variations per ad group
  • Landing page design per visa type
  • Conversion tracking setup & monitoring
  • Negative keyword management (weekly)
  • Meta Ads: audience building, creative, targeting
  • Fresh ad creative every week (platforms reward this)
  • A/B testing headlines, CTAs, images
  • Competitor ad monitoring
  • Monthly performance reporting

Create FAQ doc for common caller questions (processing times, fees, eligibility)
4
Set up call forwarding rules for after-hours and weekends
5
Review call recordings weekly for quality
6
Retrain staff every time someone leaves (every 3-6 months)
